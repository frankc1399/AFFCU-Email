Volunteer Associate Board Opportunity

The AFFCU Board of Directors is looking for volunteers in the San
Antonio area to be trained to serve on board committees and potentially
become candidates for vacant board positions in the future. The
volunteers who are selected and trained then become Associates.
The ideal Associate Board candidate requirements include:

  • Is an AFFCU Member
  • Has proven expertise in relevant fields
  • Is willing to serve and commit 3-5 hours per month
  • Has time to devote to the learning process
  • Is able to attend monthly meetings
  • Has patience for possible long-term participation on the Associate Board

If you would like to learn more about this volunteer Associate Board
opportunity, please complete the Associate Board Inquiry Form and a
Board member will contact you with further details.
